The Best Movies According to Anagha

My Top three movies of all-time.

Green Book

Dr. Don Shirley is a world-class African-American pianist who's about to embark on a concert tour in the Deep South in 1962.

Inside Out

When 11-year-old Riley moves to a new city, her Emotions team up to help her through the transition.
Joy, Fear, Anger, Disgust and Sadness work together, but when Joy and Sadness get lost,
they must journey through unfamiliar places to get back home.

Laapataa Ladies

Mistakenly swapped en route to their new husbands' homes, two brides navigate their unexpected surroundings,
making delightful self-discovery journeys.
